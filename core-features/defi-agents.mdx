## Introduction

DeFi Agents are automated programs or bots that interact with decentralized finance (DeFi) protocols to execute financial transactions or strategies on behalf of users. These agents use pre-defined rules, AI, or machine learning algorithms to handle complex workflows, optimize asset utilization, and maximize returns.

## Key Use Cases

### Portfolio Rebalancing
- Automatically adjusting asset allocations across liquidity pools or wallets
- Maintaining desired ratios based on market changes
- Real-time response to price fluctuations

### Yield Optimization
- Identifying highest-yielding opportunities
- Automated fund allocation across platforms
- Continuous APY monitoring and adjustment

### Automated Debt Management
- Monitoring collateral ratios
- Preventing liquidation through automated adjustments
- Smart collateral management

### Arbitrage Execution
- Cross-chain trading opportunities
- Multi-platform price monitoring
- Automated profit generation
