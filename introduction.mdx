## Welcome to Trikon

Trikon is a comprehensive chain abstraction platform that simplifies blockchain development with its "One Identity, One Wallet, One Balance" approach. Our platform provides seamless solutions for identity, wallet, gas, and liquidity management across multiple blockchains.
