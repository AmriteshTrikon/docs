## Platform Overview

Trikon is a chain abstraction platform that implements unified identity management, wallet operations, gas optimization, and liquidity management across multiple blockchain networks. The platform architecture follows the principle of unified blockchain interaction through standardized interfaces.

## What is Trikon?

Trikon is a comprehensive chain abstraction platform that simplifies blockchain development with its "One Identity, One Wallet, One Balance" approach. Our platform provides seamless solutions for identity, wallet, gas, and liquidity management across multiple blockchains.
